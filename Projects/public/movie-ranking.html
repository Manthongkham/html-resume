The Best Movies According to Sarawut

My Top 3 Movies

Act of Valor

An elite team of Navy SEALs embark on a covert mission to recover a kidnapped CIA agent. A look into the secretive world of the most elite, highly trained group of warriors in the modern world.

The Conjuring

The Conjuring is a 2013 supernatural horror film inspired by the true-life story of the Perron family, who claimed they "lived among the dead" in the 1970s as spirits both friendly and sinister inhabited their Rhode Island farmhouse.

Spider-man

After being bitten by a genetically-modified spider, a shy teenager gains spider-like abilities that he uses to fight injustice as a masked superhero and face a vengeful enemy.
